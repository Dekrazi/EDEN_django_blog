Welcome! I'm Dekrazi, and I am excited to share my first project with you called EDEN

EDEN is a web application that provides a platform for people to express themselves by creating and sharing blog posts.

The idea for EDEN came from my love for writing and my desire to create a space where people can share their thoughts, experiences, and ideas with others. I wanted to build a platform that was easy to use, visually appealing, and focused on the art of storytelling.

To achieve this, I chose to build EDEN using Django, a powerful and flexible web framework that makes it easy to build complex web applications. With Django, I was able to create a user-friendly interface and incorporate features such as ability to create an account, add, edit and remove posts. The goal was for the project not to be perfect but to be simple and effective.

Throughout the development of EDEN, I learned a lot about Django, web development, and project management. As a beginner I faced challenges, but I was able to overcome them with creativity and hard work.

Now that EDEN is live, I am excited to see it grow into a vibrant community of writers and readers. I believe that everyone has a story to tell and that EDEN can be a place where those stories can be shared and celebrated.

I hope you enjoy my project.
Dekrazi
